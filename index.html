Why We Are Different

Traditional vending machines are often barriers, not conveniences. Keypads are hard to read, coin slots jam, and bending down to retrieve items is difficult for many.

Pivot Vending changes that with AI Smart Cooler technology.

Visual & Clear

No tiny codes. View products and costs clearly on the screen before you buy.

No Bending Required

Grab your items from the easy-access shelf. No bending down to a low chute.

Cashless & Simple

Just tap and go. Select multiple items and pay in one single transaction.

Our Story

Pivot Vending was born from a lifetime of experience in the care sector. With over 30 years as a Midwife and Nurse, and currently operating as an SDA Provider, I understand the importance of dignity and independence.

I saw how difficult standard machines were for those with limited mobility. Pivot Vending is my solution: technology that makes the "little things" like buying a snack easy for everyone.

A Vision for Employment

We believe this technology is more than just a convenience; it is an opportunity.

Pivot Vending is actively seeking partnerships with disability organizations to explore how these easy-to-manage machines can become business opportunities for people with disabilities. The cashless, app-based tracking makes stock management accessible, opening doors for entrepreneurship.
